My Approach

Movement First

ISSA certification is built on rigorous, evidence-based curriculum grounded in exercise science, anatomy, and sport nutrition.

My certification ensures that every program I design is rooted in proven methodology — not trends or guesswork. You train with someone held to professional standards, ongoing education requirements, and full liability coverage.

About Me

Built on
hard work.

●

I've been training for over ten years, shaped by both performance-focused lifting and a deeper interest in how the body adapts, recovers, and changes over time.

That perspective became more personal after a knee injury and two surgeries, which shifted how I approach strength work — toward longevity, precision, and intelligent progression rather than intensity for its own sake.

Alongside this, my background in cell biology informs how I think about training at a systems level: adaptation, recovery, and consistency are always doing more work than extremes ever will.

I am ISSA certified (2026), and I bring that foundation into a coaching approach that is structured, evidence-based, and highly individual.

My work is built around clarity and precision. Every program is tailored — to your body, your schedule, and your capacity — with an emphasis on sustainable progress and clean execution over time.

No templates. No noise. Just focused coaching designed to move you forward, consistently.
